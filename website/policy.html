Privacy Policy

At Aaditya Buildcons, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

By using our website or engaging with our services, you consent to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. About Us

Aaditya Buildcons (formerly known as X CricStock11) is a multidisciplinary firm offering architectural design, interior design, construction, renovation, and project management services. This Privacy Policy applies to all our operations, including digital and physical interactions with clients, vendors, and partners.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following categories of information:

A. Personal Information

  • Full name, gender, date of birth
  • Email address, contact number, and postal address
  • Company or business name (if applicable)
  • Profession, designation, or client category

B. Project-Related Information

  • Property address and ownership documents
  • Design preferences, project requirements, and budgets
  • Floor plans, measurements, and site photos
  • Project progress reports and communications

C. Financial Information

  • Billing details and transaction records
  • Payment confirmations, invoices, and GST details
  • Bank account or UPI details (for project payments)

D. Technical and Usage Data

  • IP address and browser information
  • Device type and operating system
  • Website usage analytics and cookies data

3. How We Use Your Information

Aaditya Buildcons uses your information for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Providing Services: To prepare proposals, design concepts, drawings, estimates, and execute projects
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries, send updates, and coordinate ongoing projects
  • Billing & Transactions: To process payments, generate invoices, and maintain financial records
  • Project Management: To plan, track, and deliver design and construction services efficiently
  • Marketing & Promotions: To inform you about our new services, offers, events, or completed projects (only with consent)
  • Legal & Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, tax regulations, and professional standards
  • Improvement & Analytics: To analyze client preferences and improve our website's user experience

4. Sharing and Disclosure of Information

We respect your privacy and do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. However, we may share limited information with trusted third parties under the following conditions:

  • Service Providers: Contractors, consultants, and vendors engaged in your project
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law, court order, or government regulations
  • Payment Gateways: Banks or payment processors for transaction purposes
  • Marketing Partners: Only with your explicit consent, for showcasing completed projects or testimonials
  • Business Transitions: In case of mergers, acquisitions, or rebranding, your data may be transferred to the new entity

All third-party partners are obligated to maintain confidentiality and use the data solely for authorized purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include:

  • Encrypted storage and transmission of sensitive data
  • Secure payment gateways
  • Access control for staff and vendors
  • Regular data audits and backups

However, no electronic storage or transmission method is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

As a client or user, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: You may request a copy of the information we hold about you
  • Right to Correction: You may request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to Deletion: You may request deletion of your data where legally permissible
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You may withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You may object to data processing under certain conditions

7. Policy Updates

Aaditya Buildcons reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued engagement after any modification constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.
